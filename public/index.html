P'unk-Tac-Toe

Please enter your names and click 'Start Game'. Player 1 will go first and have the 'X' symbol. Player 2 will go next with the 'O' symbol. Players will alternate turns until a user has successfully aligned three of their symbols. You can start over at any point during the game by clicking the 'Reset Board' button. If you don't have a second player, you can choose to play against the computer.
